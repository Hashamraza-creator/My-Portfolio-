About Me

Hello! I'm Hasham Raza, a passionate software engineer from Multan, Pakistan. At 23 years old, I've embarked on a journey in technology driven by a deep curiosity and enthusiasm for innovation.

My Educational journey

I completed my schooling at British International School in 2016, which laid a strong foundation for my academic pursuits. Following that, I pursued my intermediate studies in FSc Pre-Engineering from Punjab College, where I developed a keen interest in technology and problem-solving. To further my knowledge and skills, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from COMSATS University Islamabad, Sahiwal Campus. During my time at COMSATS, I delved into various aspects of software development and gained hands-on experience in building scalable and efficient solutions.

Professional Experience

With a solid academic background, I have honed my skills in full-stack development, mobile app development, and various programming languages. My passion for technology extends beyond academics; I actively engage in personal projects and contribute to open-source initiatives to stay at the cutting edge of the industry.

Personal Interests

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy exploring new technologies, learning about emerging trends in software engineering, and working on creative projects. I am also passionate about traveling, which allows me to discover new places and cultures, trying out different cuisines to explore the world of flavors, and photography, which enables me to capture moments and appreciate the beauty around me.
